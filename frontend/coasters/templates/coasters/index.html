Our Experience: Building an Autonomous Etsy-Like Shop

Reflecting on our hackathon journey, we successfully created an (almost) fully autonomous Etsy-like shop specializing in custom printed mugs and custom engraved coasters. This retrospective highlights our achievements, challenges, and key learnings from the event.

A look back at our innovative hackathon project!

  • Developed a user-friendly interface that allowed customers to design their own coasters, enhancing the personalization experience.
  • Implemented automated printing and engraving processes, significantly improving production efficiency and reducing manual labor.
  • Advertised our products and made our first sales.
  • Overcame technical and organizational challenges related to the KISS-principle and what to automate, leading to a smoother customer and engineering experience.
